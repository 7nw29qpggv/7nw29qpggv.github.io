About me

Background

I am a South African born self developed multidisciplinary designer who began drawing at the age of 4... (and I am yet to stop.) Originally drawing inspiration from comic book art and vintage animation from an early age, I went on to study Graphic Design at the University of Johannesburg, graduating with a BTech Degree in 2008. My skills lay in UI/UX design, advertising, branding, photography and illustration. My focus is to deliver successful creative solutions in a perfect way through the whole process to create something unique. I am a hard working person, self motivated and ambitious to learn more.
